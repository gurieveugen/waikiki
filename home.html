Waikiki Private Hospital is an independent acute surgical and medical facility situated 45 kilometres from the central business district of Perth in the suburb of Waikiki. With 45 inpatient beds, two fully equipped theatres, an open access endoscopy unit, rehabilitation unit, sleep studies unit and on-site specialist consulting suites, Waikiki Private Hospital offers an extensive range of clinical specialties and services.

Waikiki Private Hospital is committed to providing safe, high quality health care to meet the needs of its clients and make their stay as comfortable as possible. Our facility is licensed by the WA Department of Health and implements a quality management system in accordance with International and National Quality Standards, ensuring only the highest standard of patient care is given by our staff.

The hospital has been serving the community since 1994 and is close to local shopping centres and Warnbro and Rockingham train stations. The hospital is easily accessible and provides free on-site parking.

Korn Endoscopy Unit

Waikiki Private Hospital has an open access endoscopy unit operating Monday to Friday. The unit was upgraded and in August 2014 we opened our state-of-the-art Endoscopy Suite named ‘Korn Endoscopy Unit’ in recognition of a previous patient. Contact your General Practitioner for a referral should you require these services.

Rehabilitation Unit

Waikiki Private Hospital offers a 10 bed Rehabilitation Unit for patients recovering from surgery, an accident or major illness. Our aim is to provide care and therapy that will assist patients to regain maximum independence. We have a variety of Rehabilitation Programs including Orthopaedic, Arthritis, Chronic Pain, Cardiac and Reconditioning. The Rehabilitation Unit provides access to our team of healthcare professionals including a Specialist Rehabilitation Physician, Rehabilitation Nurse, Physiotherapists and Occupational Therapist. Patients can be referred either through their Specialist or General Practitioner.

Palliative Care

The hospital offers services for end-stage palliative care. Please contact the Clinical Nurse Manager or Director of Nursing on 08 9550 0222 to enquire about a Palliative Care admission.

Sleep Studies Unit

The Waikiki Private Hospital Sleep Studies Unit offers a comprehensive service to monitor and manage people with both sleep and respiratory disorders. This service aids in the diagnosis and treatment of sleep disorders including obstructive sleep apnoea (OSA), insomnia, periodic limb movements (PLM’s), narcolepsy and hypoventilation syndromes.

During a sleep study, a patient spends the night in a private room in hospital connected to a variety of monitoring equipment, observed by qualified sleep technologists. Their sleep is analysed for evidence of sleep apnoea and/or other sleep disorders which may be causing daytime tiredness and fatigue. In certain patient groups, portable or home based sleep studies may also be recommended.

If you suspect you have a sleep or respiratory disorder, the first step is to consult with your GP who will advise if further, more specialised assessment is required. To have a sleep study or to see a specialist, a referral from your GP is required. If you have a referral and need to book a sleep study or require further information please contact 1300 570 700 or Sleep WA.
